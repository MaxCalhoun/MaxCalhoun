Kiwi & the Nightmare Circus

My team and I designed Kiwi & the Nightmare Circus for the ZX Spectrum and developed it in Unity. The gameplay, story and artstyle are deeply inspired by the games available on the system. We even made use of a library that mimics the attribute clash from the Spectrum in Unity.
