Operation TP

Operation TP is a gameboy game reflecting on the events of 2020, developed in GB Studio. The player is given a quest to pickup some toilet paper, a once abundent item. To obtain this commodity, the main character must complete several side tasks/minigames. I designed and implemented two of these. The first is a driving mini-game, where the player must navigate heavy traffic during their journey to the supermarket.


The second minigame is a puzzle. When the player arrives at the store, they are awaited by a long line of customers. At this point the main character has several means of entering the shop. They may bribe the customers in-line, find a secret entrance beside the building, or win a game or rock, paper, scissors against the security-guard.
