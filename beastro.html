Beastro Brunch, Lunch, 'N' Dinner!?!

In Beastro Brunch, Lunch, 'N' Dinner!?!, you play as a restaurant owner trying to make a name for herself. During business hours, customers will dine-in, requesting a specific meal. The character cooks the meal in the kitchen using their stockpiled ingredients, and must deliver it to the diner in a timely manner. There are two ways of obtaining ingredients. You can grow them on the farm by planting seeds, or you can venture into the wilderness and hunt the roaming monsters.

While building Beastro, I contributed to several aspects of the game. In the begining, I worked on the character movement and animations, and the healing and dodging parts of the combat system. Afterwards I shifted to creating additional content such as item pickups and enemies. At the end of the development cycle I implemented the save system for the game.
