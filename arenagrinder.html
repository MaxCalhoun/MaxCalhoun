Arena Grinder

Arena Grinder is a game I created in javascript using the p5.js editor and p5.play library. I designed this game to demonstrate the balance between paying everyday expenses and debt. When you start the game, you have a debt of 100 gold coins. Your job is to fight combatents in the arena to earn gold. you can either spend the gold you earn on buying potions to keep yourself alive, or knocking out a small portion of your debt. Your debt will increase after each round due to interest. Your deficit will grow if you spend buy too many potions, and you won't be able to survive if you pay too much of your debt at once. However, with the right balance of spending you can and will become debt free.
