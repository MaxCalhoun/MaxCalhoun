Cryptids Inn Love

Cryptids Inn Love is a game several students and I came up with for a game jam. You buy cryptids to raise and take care of in your hotel. The player must ensure their cryptids are happy and healthy, otherwise they will pass away. Cryptids can also develop relationships with eachother. If two are on the same floor, their relationship meter will slowly rise. Once the relationship bar is full, you can click a button to breed them. The newly created child will bear features from either parent at random. For this project, I implemented the relationship mechanics between cryptids as well as the cryptid movement and placement within the hotel. It was build using Unity.
